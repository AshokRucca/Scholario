Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my eligibility?

Simply create an account and fill out our multi-step verification wizard. We'll instantly match your profile against our database to show you which scholarships you qualify for.

Is this platform free to use?

Yes, our core platform for students is completely free. We believe every student should have access to financial aid information without barriers.

Are the scholarships verified?

Absolutely. We source our data directly from official government portals (like NSP), trusted NGOs, and partnered corporate foundations. Every listing undergoes a strict verification process.
